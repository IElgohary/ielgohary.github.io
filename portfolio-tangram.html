As part of the backend team at dlc., I worked on Tangram which is an ERP and CRM for tourism companies. I was part of the team responsible for maintaining the product and updating the server-side logic.

Tangram ERP is composed of 6 correlated Modules:
1. Sales.
2. HR.
3. Accounting.
4. Operations.
5. Contracting / fleet management module
6. Reservation Module
